Microwave Mug Cookie

Description

Its a cookie in a Mug!! What more could you want? Pretty sure this could solve wars.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon (15g) salted butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon light brown sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 heaped tablespoons plain flour, heat-treated
  • 1 tablespoon chocolate chips, plus extra for topping

Steps

  1. In a microwave-safe bowl, add 1 tbsp salted butter.
  2. Melt the butter for 20 seconds in a microwave oven.
  3. Then, stir until fully melted.
  4. Add 1 tbsp granulated sugar and 1 tbsp light brown sugar to the melted butter and mix well.
  5. Add the egg yolk and 1/2 tsp vanilla extract to the butter and mix until combined.
  6. Add the 2 heaped tbsp plain flour to the mixture and stir until combined.
  7. Then, add the 1 tbsp chocolate chips to the mixture.
  8. Mix it well and make it thin.
  9. Add chocolate chips to the top of the mixture for topping.
  10. Microwave on medium (500W) for 40-50 seconds. (Be careful not to overcook it to prevent it from becoming dry.)
  11. Let it cool slightly before serving.
  12. Enjoy your delicious microwave mug cookie.
